HOW TO CLEAR BROWSER CACHE
====================================

The CSS changes have been applied, but your browser may be showing old cached styles.
Please follow these steps to see the fixes:

WINDOWS:
--------
Chrome/Edge: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete OR Ctrl + F5
Firefox: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete OR Ctrl + F5
Opera: Press Ctrl + F5

ALTERNATIVE METHOD (Works for all browsers):
-------------------------------------------
1. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
2. Right-click on the Refresh button
3. Select "Empty Cache and Hard Reload"

OR

Simply close the browser completely and reopen it, then visit:
http://localhost:8000
